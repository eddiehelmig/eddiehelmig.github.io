Designer's Statement

I'm a digital artist and photographer with a background in graphic design and visual storytelling. My creative process combines traditional design principles with modern digital tools, primarily Procreate, Photoshop, and Illustrator, to create artwork that bridges emotion and structure.

"My work lives at the intersection of color, form, and feeling — using visual rhythm and minimalism to communicate meaning."

Whether I'm developing a clean vector illustration, composing a surreal photo edit, or capturing a quiet natural moment through photography, I aim to evoke a sense of clarity, presence, and atmosphere. Every piece begins with an emotional intent and resolves through deliberate compositional choices.

Professional Goals

My immediate goal is to continue developing my visual identity as a graphic designer and artist, while expanding my technical and conceptual range. I'm currently pursuing an Associate of Fine Arts at Delaware County Community College, building a portfolio that highlights storytelling, composition, and originality.

This portfolio represents a cross-section of my strongest work: from digital paintings and illustrations to photography and compositing. It reflects both my creative growth and my ongoing pursuit of excellence in design.

Tools & Mediums

Procreate for digital painting and illustration · Adobe Photoshop for compositing and photo editing · Adobe Illustrator for vector work and poster design · Lightroom for photography and color grading.
